About ekoloko

ekoloko was developed to meet the needs of kids online, by providing them with quality content that promotes values and knowledge. ekoloko offers a safe and controlled environment, featuring a wide range of adventures and activities, and a community aspect that allows kids to advance socially by taking more responsibility and making significant contributions to the ekoloko experience. All the narratives in ekoloko are based on environmental and social themes and demonstrate how kids can help protect the environment in their own personal lives.

ekoloko has been a smashing success in Israel with over 1.5 million registered users and about 250,000 active users every month!

Content with value
  • Kids enter an overriding storyline of good vs. bad where they join the good guys and protect ekoloko from the bad guys.
  • All narratives are based on environmental and social themes.
  • There is a wide range of activities to choose from so that kids of all ages, gender and personalities will find something that they can relate to.
A dynamic platform
  • ekoloko is not a game -it is a dynamic environment where kids can enjoy a different experience every day. New content is introduced on a continuous basis. In-world "counselors" are online at any given time and lead fun activities for the kids.
  • The platform is built to provide kids with a safe and controlled experience. We deploy sophisticated technology and live monitoring to ensure that kids don't provide personal information or use language that could be offensive.
A growing community
  • Kids in ekoloko are able to advance not only on an individual level but also on a social level. Kids are encouraged to become Rangers and take more responsibility in the community.
  • ekoloko maintains a strong connection with the "real world", providing kids with content that deals with real-life issues and teaching them how to make a difference in their own personal environment.
